Reading announcements

You can read announcements from within each site to which you belong. Enter a site, and from the menubar click Announcements. To read an announcement, click its title.

From My Workspace, you can view a consolidated list of announcements from all your sites. Enter My Workspace, and from the menubar click Announcements. To read an announcement, click its title.

You can sort the announcements list by clicking on any of the column headings (e.g., Subject, Date, etc.) to sort by that column. Clicking the same heading again will reverse the sorting order.
