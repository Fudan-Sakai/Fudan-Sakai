Using Schedule Summary

A summarized version of the Schedule tool appears on the Home page of individual worksites. Site participants can see a summary of upcoming scheduled events. Days that contain events are highlighted.

Navigation

  • Use the left and right arrows to change months or weeks. Click Today to return to the current day.

  • Click a day to see a summary of events for that day.

  • Click the title of an event to see a summary of that event.

  • Click Full details... to go to the Schedule tool and view complete details for the event.

Options

Click Options to set your preferences, including:

  • Calendar display: Next to "View", use the drop-down list to select either Calendar by Week or Calendar by Month.

  • Event priority: Under "Priority events", define event priorities and priority colors. Highlight an event type and click Move up or Move down to set the priority to high, medium, or low.

  • Colors: Enter hexadecimal values or click the Color Palette icon to select colors to represent high, medium, and low priority events.

To save your changes, click Update. To cancel, click Cancel.
