Moving or copying Drop Box items to another folder

To move or copy item(s) from one Drop Box folder to another:

  1. In the menubar of the relevant site, click Drop Box.

  2. Click the black triangles to the left of "Title" to expand all the folders in the Drop Box, or click the folder icon to the left of the name of the folder you wish to expand. (If a folder contains items not currently displayed, a plus sign will appear on the folder icon.)

  3. Check the box next to the item(s), and click Move or Copy.

  4. Click the "Paste copied items" icon ( 
     ) next to the folder in which you want to paste the item(s), or from the Actions menu, choose Paste copied items.
