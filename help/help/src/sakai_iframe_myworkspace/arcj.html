Joining and unjoining sites

To join a site:

  1. In My Workspace, from the menubar, click Membership.

  2. Click Joinable Sites to see a list of all the available sites that you haven't joined. If you'd like to join any of them, click Join.

  3. To search for a particular site or group of sites:

    1. Enter your search terms in the Search box.
    2. Click Search. Simply pressing Enter will not initiate the search.
    3. Click Join to join the site you were searching for.
    4. After you click Join, you will get the following message: "There are no joinable sites matching your search of 'search term' ", where "search term" represents the search term(s) you entered. However, you will see the site you just joined if you click  - more -  at the top of the page.

To unjoin a site:

  1. In My Workspace, from the menubar, click Membership.

  2. Under "My Current Sites", you'll see a list of all the sites to which you belong. (This includes sites you have joined, and sites that you have been added to as a participant by the creator of the site.) If you'd like to unjoin any of them, click Unjoin.
