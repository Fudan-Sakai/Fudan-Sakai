Emailing forum message authors

In Forums, certain roles can directly email the author of a message in a forum. To do so:

  1. In your site's menubar, click Forums.

  2. Click the title of the forum, and then the title of the relevant topic.

  3. Click the title of a message or thread. While looking at an individual message, click Email to send email to the author's authorized email address. When looking at a thread, each message in the thread will have an Email link under it; clicking the link will send email to the author of that message.
