Design and Publish
This area includes tools where you can select and arrange items you have collected in Resources into a Portfolio. You can choose to design the look and feel of your Portfolio entirely on your own or you can use a set of pre-defined templates. Once you have completed your Portfolio, you can publish your Portfolio to a set of users such as all faculty, to an individual user, or even to the public through a publicly accessible URL or via a CD-ROM.
