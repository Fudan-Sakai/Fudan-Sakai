Editing information about the RSS feed in Podcasts

In Podcasts, to change the title and description of the RSS feed:

  1. From your site's menubar, click Podcasts.

  2. Under the RSS feed URL, click Edit.

  3. You can change the title and description of the RSS feed, which will be available to your site participants through their podcatcher applications.

  4. When you're finished, click Save Changes.
