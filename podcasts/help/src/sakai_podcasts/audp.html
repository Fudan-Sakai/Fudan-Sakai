Managing access to podcasts

Note: To complete these procedures, you must be assigned a role having the necessary permissions. To determine your role, follow the directions in Participant roles. For a basic understanding of roles and permissions, see Permissions and roles: Overview.

With the Podcasts tool, you can restrict access to your podcasts to site participants only, or you can make them available to the public. You can also edit permissions, and define who is allowed to create, revise, and delete podcasts.

Managing access to your podcasts

  1. From your site's menubar, click Podcasts.

  2. Near the top, click Options, and choose one of the following:

    • To provide public access to your podcasts through a podcatcher, choose Display to non-members (publicly viewable).
    • To restrict access to site participants only, and to disable access through a podcatcher, choose Display to site.

  3. When you're finished, click Save Changes.

Note: This will affect access to all of your podcasts.

Editing permissions

To edit permissions from within the Podcasts tool:

  1. From your site's menubar, click Podcasts.

  2. Near the top, click Permissions.

  3. On the Permissions screen, you can add permissions based on participant roles. Permissions that are set in Resources cannot be changed in the Podcasts tool. Available permissions are as follows:

    content.new Add new podcasts
    content.read View or download podcasts
    content.revise.any Edit podcast information for all podcasts
    content.revise.own Edit podcast information for your podcasts
    content.delete.any Remove any podcasts
    content.delete.own Remove your own podcasts
    content.all.groups View all podcasts, even those restricted to certain groups
    content.hidden View all podcasts regardless of show/hide settings on each

  4. When you're finished, click Save.

To edit permissions that are set in the Resources tool:

  1. From your site's menubar, click Resources.

  2. On the Resources page, click Permissions.

    Note: Permission changes made from this location will affect all items in the Resources tool. To edit permissions for an individual folder, see Changing the permissions for a folder in Resources

  3. When you're finished, click Save.
