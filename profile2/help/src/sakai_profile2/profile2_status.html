Profile2: Updating your status

Status updates are a good way to let other people know what you are up to or what's on your mind.

To post a status update

  1. On your Profile page, type something in the status box.
  2. Click Say it to post the update.

To remove a status update

  1. Click Clear next to your status message.

Status updates will also disappear when they are about a week old.

Status updates can also be sent to your Twitter account, if you have one. You can manage this in your Preferences page.

You can also restrict who can see your status updates in your Privacy page.
