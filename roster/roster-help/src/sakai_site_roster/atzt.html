Printing your roster

To print your roster:

  1. In your site's menubar, click Roster.

  2. Click Print in the upper-right corner of the Roster screen.

  3. Your browser's print dialog box will appear. Adjust your print settings as needed, and then click Print or OK.

To print your roster with participants' pictures:

  1. In your site's menubar, click Roster.

  2. Click Pictures.

  3. To print the screen as it appears, in the upper right, click Print Screen. To print all pictures in a single column, click Print Pictures in Single Column.

  4. Your browser's print dialog box will appear. Adjust your print settings as needed, and then click Print or OK.
