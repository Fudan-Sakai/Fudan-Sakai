Viewing site information

In a site's menubar, click Site Info to view information about the site.

  • If your role has restricted permissions for the site (e.g., participants, students, etc.), you will see information published by the site's owner as well as any group memberships, if applicable.

  • If your role includes a broad level of permissions (e.g., site managers, instructors, etc.), you will see a participant list as well as tools to edit that list and other site information. For editing instructions, see:

    • Editing information about your site
    • Adding, editing, or removing participants
    • Adding, removing, reordering, renaming, or hiding tools
