Printing your syllabus

To create a printable version of your syllabus and print it:

  1. In your site's menubar, click Syllabus.

  2. To view a printable version of the page, at the top, click the Printer icon.

  3. While viewing the printable version, click Send To Printer.

  4. Your browser's print dialog box will appear. Adjust your print settings as needed, and then click OK.
